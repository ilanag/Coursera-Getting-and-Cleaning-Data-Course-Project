All Variables have a min value of -1 and a max value of 1. The value corresponds to the reading of
device from which this variable was measured (i.e. accelerometer and gyroscope). The "f" designation
indicated "frequency" and the "t" designation indicates "time".The variables are listed below, and
follow the naming conventions detailed above. 

tBodyAccMeanX: Mean of body acceleration on the X axis.
tBodyAccMeanY: Mean of body acceleration on the y axis.
tBodyAccMeanZ: Mean of body acceleration on the z axis.
tGravityAccMeanX: Mean of gravity acceleration on the X axis.
tGravityAccMeanY: Mean of gravity acceleration on the y axis.
tGravityAccMeanZ: Mean of gravity acceleration on the z axis.
tBodyAccJerkMeanX: Mean of body jerk acceleration on the X axis.
tBodyAccJerkMeanY: Mean of body jerk acceleration on the y axis.
tBodyAccJerkMeanZ: Mean of body jerk acceleration on the z axis.
tBodyGyroMeanX: Mean of body velocity on the X axis.
tBodyGyroMeanY: Mean of body velocity on the y axis.
tBodyGyroMeanZ: Mean of body velocity on the z axis.
tBodyGyroJerkMeanX: Mean of body jerk velocity on the X axis.
tBodyGyroJerkMeanY: Mean of body jerk velocity on the y axis.
tBodyGyroJerkMeanZ: Mean of body jerk velocity on the z axis.
tBodyAccMagMean: Mean of body acceleration magnitude.
tGravityAccMagMean: Mean of gravity acceleration magnitude.
tBodyAccJerkMagMean: Mean of body jerk acceleration magnitude.
tBodyGyroMagMean: Mean of body velocity magnitude.
tBodyGyroJerkMagMean: Mean of body jerk velocity magnitude.
fBodyAccMeanX: Mean of body acceleration on the X axis.
fBodyAccMeanY: Mean of body acceleration on the y axis.
fBodyAccMeanZ: Mean of body acceleration on the z axis.
fBodyAccMeanFreqX: Mean of body acceleration frequency on the X axis.
fBodyAccMeanFreqY: Mean of body acceleration frequency on the y axis.
fBodyAccMeanFreqZ: Mean of body acceleration frequency on the z axis.
fBodyAccJerkMeanX: Mean of body acceleration on the X axis.
fBodyAccJerkMeanY: Mean of body acceleration on the y axis.
fBodyAccJerkMeanZ: Mean of body acceleration on the z axis.
fBodyAccJerkMeanFreqX: Mean of body jerk acceleration frequency on the X axis.
fBodyAccJerkMeanFreqY: Mean of body jerk acceleration frequency on the y axis.
fBodyAccJerkMeanFreqZ: Mean of body jerk acceleration frequency on the z axis.
fBodyGyroMeanX: Mean of body velocity on the X axis.
fBodyGyroMeanY: Mean of body velocity on the y axis.
fBodyGyroMeanZ: Mean of body velocity on the z axis.
fBodyGyroMeanFreqX: Mean of body velocity frequency on the X axis.
fBodyGyroMeanFreqY: Mean of body velocity frequency on the y axis.
fBodyGyroMeanFreqZ: Mean of body velocity frequency on the z axis.
fBodyAccMagMean: Mean of body acceleration magnitude.
fBodyAccMagMeanFreq: Mean of body acceleration frequency magnitude.
fBodyAccJerkMagMean: Mean of body acceleration jerk magnitude.
fBodyAccJerkMagMeanFreq: Mean of body acceleration jerk frequency.
fBodyGyroMagMean: Mean of body velocity magnitude.
fBodyGyroMagMeanFreq: Mean of body velocity frequency magnitude.
fBodyGyroJerkMagMean: Mean of body jerk velocity magnitude.
fBodyGyroJerkMagMeanFreq: Mean of body jerk velocity frequency magnitude.
tBodyAccStdX: Standard deviation of body acceleration on the X axis.
tBodyAccStdY: Standard deviation of body acceleration on the y axis.
tBodyAccStdZ: Standard deviation of body acceleration on the z axis.
tGravityAccStdX: Standard deviation of gravity acceleration on the X axis.
tGravityAccStdY: Standard deviation of gravity acceleration on the y axis.
tGravityAccStdZ: Standard deviation of gravity acceleration on the z axis.
tBodyAccJerkStdX: Standard deviation of body jerk acceleration on the X axis.
tBodyAccJerkStdY: Standard deviation of body jerk acceleration on the y axis.
tBodyAccJerkStdZ: Standard deviation of body jerk acceleration on the z axis.
tBodyGyroStdX: Standard deviation of body velocity on the X axis.
tBodyGyroStdY: Standard deviation of body velocity on the y axis.
tBodyGyroStdZ: Standard deviation of body velocity on the z axis.
tBodyGyroJerkStdX: Standard deviation of body jerk velocity on the X axis.
tBodyGyroJerkStdY: Standard deviation of body jerk velocity on the y axis.
tBodyGyroJerkStdZ: Standard deviation of body jerk velocity on the z axis.
tBodyAccMagStd: Standard deviation of body acceleration magnitude.
tGravityAccMagStd: Standard deviation of gravity acceleration magnitude.
tBodyAccJerkMagStd: Standard deviation of body jerk acceleration magnitude.
tBodyGyroMagStd: Standard deviation of body velocity magnitude.
tBodyGyroJerkMagStd: Standard deviation of body jerk velocity magnitude.
fBodyAccStdX: Standard deviation of body acceleration on the X axis.
fBodyAccStdY: Standard deviation of body acceleration on the y axis.
fBodyAccStdZ: Standard deviation of body acceleration on the z axis.
fBodyAccJerkStdX: Standard deviation of body jerk acceleration on the X axis.
fBodyAccJerkStdY: Standard deviation of body jerk acceleration on the y axis.
fBodyAccJerkStdZ: Standard deviation of body jerk acceleration on the z axis.
fBodyGyroStdX: Standard deviation of body velocity on the X axis.
fBodyGyroStdY: Standard deviation of body velocity on the y axis.
fBodyGyroStdZ: Standard deviation of body velocity on the z axis.
fBodyAccMagStd: Standard deviation of body acceleration magnitude.
fBodyAccJerkMagStd: Standard deviation of body jerk acceleration magnitude.
fBodyGyroMagStd: Standard deviation of body velocity magnitude.
fBodyGyroJerkMagStd: Standard deviation of body jerk velocity magnitude.
